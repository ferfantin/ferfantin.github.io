What We Do
In the pursuit of challenging ourselves and testing out our 2D skills, we have been developing small game projects in our free time. Currently, we are focused into developing Cold Snap, a pixel art 2D plataformer game where the main character, a winter spirit, rushes to bring the cold season to the Earth when its due time.
Concept and Early Prototypes
Cold Snap
Cold Snap is a small game project being developed using mainly Godot and Aseprite. Implementation and iteration are done after design and the required assets have been planned. The biggest challenges in this project are coding and achivable game design since our daily jobs are art focused. Thus, it is a fun and exciting challenge to overcome!
Who Are We?
We are a couple developing games and making art for a living. After graduating from university in Brazil, we moved to Japan to work and now, since 2020, we have been living in the UK. Danilo is an UI Designer that makes websites and pixel art in his free time. Fernanda is an UI Artist that loves to create creepy & cute illustrations.
